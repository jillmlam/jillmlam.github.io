About Jill Lamkins

Hello my name is Jill Lamkins. This is a project for my Sports Data & Analysis class. I am a junior broadcasting and sports media double major with a minor in psychology. I am a member of the Scarlets Dance Team, Kappa Alpha Theta and Nebraska Nightly. In this course I have learned how to create code using RStudios. In the final stretch of the class, we are doing this project to put the skills we have learned to the test. While I wouldn’t say coding is my specialty, I am always up to learn new skills to further me in my future endeavors. I am always up for a challenge and enjoy working with others to create solutions whenever a problem arises. I have worked hard on this project. Enjoy.
